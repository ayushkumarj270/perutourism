Popular Attractions in Peru

Explore the iconic landmarks and attractions across Peru's most famous cities.

Explore Our Attractions

Plaza Mayor

The central square of Lima, featuring historic buildings and a vibrant atmosphere.

Opening Hours: 24 hours

Huaca Pucllana

An ancient archaeological site in the heart of Lima, with pyramid structures and ancient artifacts.

Opening Hours: Daily, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Parque Kennedy

A bustling park in the Miraflores district, surrounded by cafes, shops, and local art.

Opening Hours: 24 hours

Sacsayhuamán

An ancient Inca ceremonial complex with impressive stonework and panoramic views of Cusco.

Opening Hours: Daily, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Sacred Valley

A picturesque valley with Inca ruins, colonial towns, and stunning landscapes.

Opening Hours: 24 hours

Qorikancha (Temple of the Sun)

An Inca temple, once covered in gold, that is now a museum showcasing ancient artifacts.

Opening Hours: Daily, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Santa Catalina Monastery

A stunning monastery complex with colorful streets and historic architecture.

Opening Hours: Daily, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Colca Canyon

One of the deepest canyons in the world, offering breathtaking views and hiking opportunities.

Opening Hours: Daily, 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Arequipa’s Historic Center

A UNESCO World Heritage site with colonial architecture and lively plazas.

Opening Hours: 24 hours

Lake Titicaca

The highest navigable lake in the world, with floating islands and traditional communities.

Opening Hours: Daily, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Sillustani Tombs

Ancient burial towers on a hill overlooking Lake Umayo.

Opening Hours: Daily, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Puno Cathedral

A beautiful baroque-style cathedral located in Puno’s main square.

Opening Hours: Daily, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Manatee Rescue Center

A sanctuary for rescued Amazonian manatees, offering educational tours and wildlife conservation efforts.

Opening Hours: Daily, 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Belén Market

A bustling local market offering exotic fruits, medicinal plants, and Amazonian goods.

Opening Hours: Daily, 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Amazon River Cruise

A scenic boat trip down the Amazon River, exploring the unique wildlife and communities.

Opening Hours: Daily, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
